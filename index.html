Tours to Trælanípa & Sørvágsvatn

The majestic cliff of Trælanípa, rising dramatically from the ocean, offers a unique optical illusion where the serene Lake Sørvágsvatn appears to hover above the sea. Hike along the scenic trails and witness panoramic views that blend lush green landscapes with the rugged North Atlantic coastline.

A visit to Trælanípa promises an unforgettable adventure, combining natural wonder with the timeless allure of the Faroese wilderness. Don't miss the chance to experience this enchanting sight on your Faroe Islands journey!

Bøsdalafossur is just a stone's throw away!

Marvel at the waters of Lake Sørvágsvatn cascading directly into the Atlantic Ocean in a breathtaking spectacle.

Guided tours of Tórshavn

Explore the picturesque capital of the Faroe Islands. Wander through its colorful, turf-roofed houses in the historic Tinganes district, where Viking history comes alive. Enjoy the vibrant cultural scene, with cozy cafes, local boutiques, and inspiring art galleries. Stroll along the bustling harbor and savor fresh seafood at exquisite restaurants. Tórshavn offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and rich heritage. Come and experience the heart of Faroese culture in this captivating town!

  • Explore the town's rich history

  • Pay a visit to the local artisan shops
